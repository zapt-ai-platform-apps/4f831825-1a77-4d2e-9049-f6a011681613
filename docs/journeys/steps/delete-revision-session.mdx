- **Identify the Session to Delete**:
  - In the **Revision Sessions** section, find the session you want to remove.
- **Click the Delete Button**:
  - Click the **Delete** button next to the session.
- **Confirm Deletion**:
  - A confirmation prompt will appear.
  - Click **OK** to confirm and delete the session.
  - The session will be removed from the list and the timetable.
