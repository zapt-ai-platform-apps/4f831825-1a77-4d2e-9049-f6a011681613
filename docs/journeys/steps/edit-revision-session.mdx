- **Identify the Session to Edit**:
  - In the **Revision Sessions** section of the **Details** panel, locate the session you wish to edit.
- **Click the Edit Button**:
  - Click the **Edit** button associated with that session.
- **Modify Session Details**:
  - The session details will populate the **Edit Revision Session** form.
  - Update the fields as needed.
- **Save Changes**:
  - Click the **Update Session** button to save your changes.
  - The session details will be updated in the list and on the timetable.
