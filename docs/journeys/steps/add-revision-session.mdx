- **Locate the Revision Session Form**:
  - Scroll to the **Add Revision Session** section in the **Details** panel.
- **Fill in the Session Details**:
  - **Block**:
    - Select the time block (Morning, Afternoon, Evening) for your session.
  - **Subject**:
    - Enter the subject you plan to study.
  - **Start Time** and **End Time**:
    - Specify the exact start and end times for the session.
- **Save the Session**:
  - Click the **Add Session** button.
  - The new session will appear in the list of sessions for that day and on the timetable.
